How to Use the Expense Tracker

Follow these simple steps to start tracking your income and expenses:

  1. Select your preferred currency (USD or INR).
  2. Add a transaction by entering a description, selecting the type (Income/Expense), choosing a category, and entering the amount.
  3. View your transactions in the table below.
  4. Use the chart to visualize your income and expenses.
  5. Download your financial report or chart for offline use.
